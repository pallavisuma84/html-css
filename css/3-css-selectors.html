About Charminar

History

"The Charminar is monument and mosque in Hyderabad, India. The structure was built in 1591 AD,It is the most famous building of Hyderabad and also one of the most famous buildings in India,It was built by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shahi to celebrate the end of deadly plague,It is close to Laad Bazaar and Makkah Masjid"

Location

Charminar means four minarets is beautiful piece of architecture located in Hyderabad in Telangana, India, It is located at the heart of Hyderabad city. It is one of the most famous historical monuments of India, It was built by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah in 1591, Some believe that it was built to celebrate the end of deadly plague, Charminar is known as “Arc De Triumph of the East” symbolises Hyderabad
